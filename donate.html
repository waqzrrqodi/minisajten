Donate

Donate to help us keep the site running and to help us expand our content.

Donations are not required, but are appreciated.

Thank you for your support!

PayPal

Donate using PayPal. Donating using PayPal is a quick and easy way for individuals to make financial contributions to charitable organizations and non-profits. PayPal is an online payment system that allows users to securely transfer money from their bank account or credit card to the organization of their choice.

Patreon

Donate using Patreon. Donating using Patreon is a way for individuals to financially support creators and artists on a recurring basis. Patreon is a platform that allows creators to set up a "membership" style system, where patrons (or donors) can choose to pledge a certain amount of money per creation, or on a monthly basis.

Personal Data

Donate using Personal Data. Donating using personal data is a relatively new concept that involves using data, such as browsing history or social media activity, to tailor donation campaigns and target specific groups of people. By analyzing data, organizations can identify potential donors who are more likely to be interested in their cause and tailor their messaging and donation requests to appeal to them.

Other

Donate using other methods. Donating by participating in a charity run or walk -Donating by purchasing a product from a company that gives a portion of its profits to charity -Donating by rounding up your purchase to the nearest dollar at certain retailers -Donating your frequent flyer miles -Donating your time by volunteering at a charity event -Donating by purchasing a charity gift card Keep in mind that when donating, it's important to research the organization and ensure that your donation is going towards a reputable cause.
